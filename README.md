# The FREEDOM Framework

**Declaration for a Free World Order**

[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-blue.svg)](LICENSE)
[![Latest Release](https://img.shields.io/github/v/release/dornori/freedom-7-pillars)](https://github.com/dornori/freedom-7-pillars/releases)

**The FREEDOM Framework** is a collaborative, inclusive declaration for a free world order.  
It presents **7 foundational pillars** as a neutral system to support human flourishing — open to all perspectives and cosmologies.

### The FREEDOM Pillar System

| Letter | Pillar          | Description |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| **F**  | **Foundation**  | Food, water, shelter, energy – the essential base for life. Inviolable lifetime land allocation. |
| **R**  | **Resilience**  | Human continuity and disaster recovery – protection against earthquakes, global warming, and existential threats. |
| **E**  | **Exchange**    | The human-exchange layer: universal platform for connection, expression, advertising, trade, collaboration, communication, and exchange of valuables. Free internet, personal data ownership, freedom of movement. |
| **E**  | **Equilibrium** | Free universal healthcare and pharmaceuticals, physical and mental care, sanitation – balanced well-being of body and mind. |
| **D**  | **Defense**     | Right to defend or be defended. Right to fair trial, safety, rule of law, stability – protection from inequality, unfair treatment, harm and chaos. |
| **O**  | **Ownership**   | Autonomy, knowledge, meaningful choice – personal control over one’s life and decisions. |
| **M**  | **Mobility**    | Freedom of movement and privacy – the right to travel and exist without forced identification when no crime or threat is present. |

## 📖 About The FREEDOM Framework

This living document offers a structured yet flexible declaration for organizing human society. It is designed as an open foundation that welcomes contributions from all worldviews and does not exclude any cosmology or perspective.
